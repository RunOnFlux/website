FLUX ECOSYSTEM

The sovereign
new Internet

The Flux ecosystem is made up of a number of different moving parts. FluxOS and FluxNodes provide the computational network, zelcore is the wallet with built in apps to power the wider ecosystem, the Flux blockchain is used to fund the ecosystem and the Flux parallel assets provide interoperability with other blockchains.

Decentralized

Interoperable

Redundant

FLUXNODES  DISTRIBUTION

Scalable and decentralized
computational network

The FluxOS is powered by individual Flux holders running FluxNodes. These FluxNodes are geographically-spaced all over the world, so developers can be assured that their apps are accessible by users at all times and locations.

Enterprise grade

The majority of FluxNodes are hosted in professional datacenters all over the globe giving high uptimes and bandwidth.

Scalable

FluxNodes must meet certain minimum requirements to launch ensuring a predictable level of performance for applications running on the nodes.

Rewards

FluxNode operators are rewarded daily for running a node with block rewards being split 50/50 between miners and node operators.

Global

FluxNodes are run in dozens of countries around the world providing a truly distributed computational platform.
